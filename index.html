Andrew strives to protect and understand life. He professionally develops optimization models of biological metabolism that can expand basic understanding of life processes and can foster technologies in synthetic biology and the rational design of microbial communities. These insights can be applied to resolve myriad challenges in social sustainability and human health.

His professional career is detailed in his Curriculum Vitae, scholarly publications, and conference presentations. His personal explorations in dietary health are summarized in Reviews on this site.

Andrew spends his free time volunteering and pursuing entrepreneurial opportunities.
