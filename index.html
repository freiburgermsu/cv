Andrew admires the natural world, and strives to protect and understand life. He personally advocates for veganism & minimalism as small habits that conserve the ecosystem while improving one's health and financial security, respectively. He professionally researches technologies that may improve the sustainability of society and elucidate the mysteries of biology: e.g. plant replacements for petroleum, seawater desalination, light-activated antibiotics, and metabolic modeling understanding and designing biological solutions to challenges in sustainability and health.

His research and professional career are expressed in his Curriculum Vitae, scholarly publications, and conferences presentations. His personal research in dietary health is summarized in reviews on this site.

Andrew spends his free time practicing Tai Chi Praying Mantis Kung Fu; playing guitar/bass/singing with bandmates; trading in the NYSE; volunteering in scientific endeavors; and pursuing startup opportunities.
