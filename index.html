Andrew admires the natural world, and strives to protect and understand life. He personally advocates for veganism minimalism as personal habits that improve one's health and financial security, respectively, while altruistically conserving ecosystems and animal lives. He professionally researches technologies that extend these aspirations: e.g. improving the sustainability of society through seawater desalination, light-activated antibiotics, and plant replacements for petroleum; and elucidating the mysteries of biology through metabolic modeling that can foster synthetic biology and the rational design of biological solutions to challenges in sustainability and health.

His research and professional career are embodied in his Curriculum Vitae, scholarly publications, and conference presentations. His personal explorations in dietary health are summarized in Reviews on this site.

Andrew spends his free time practicing Martial Arts (Tai Chi Praying Mantis Kung Fu and Krav Maga); playing guitar, bass, and singing with bandmates; investing and trading in the NYSE; volunteering; and pursuing startup opportunities.
